FAQs

What is ThermoLink, and how does it work?
ThermoLink is an innovative solution that captures excess heat from data centers and converts it into sustainable energy. Using advanced thermal systems, we repurpose waste heat to warm homes and businesses efficiently.
Is ThermoLink environmentally friendly?
Absolutely! By repurposing heat that would otherwise go to waste, ThermoLink reduces carbon emissions and promotes cleaner energy usage, contributing to a more sustainable planet.
Can ThermoLink help businesses save costs?
Yes, ThermoLink offers cost-effective solutions by transforming waste heat into reusable energy. This reduces operational energy costs for businesses and promotes efficiency.
